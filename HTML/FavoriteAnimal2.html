Information About Pugs



Pug dogs are the whole package; cute and sociable with a fun loving personality, they make great companions and lovable pets. Pugs have glossy coats that come in many colors, the most common ones being fawn or black. Their wrinkled faces and curly tails make them extra cute and lovable! On top of their easy appearance, pugs have unique personalities and are known for being social and gentle dogs.
