Malala Yousafzai

Education's Courageous Advocate

Biography

Malala Yousafzai is a 20-year-old education activist from Swat, Pakistan.
She is well known for her action taken against Taliban, who attempted to murder her for speaking up for women's and children's rights.
Malala survived the attempt, moved to England and continued to fight for the right to education through speeches and literature.
